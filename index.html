V&A Heating

Origin Of The Company

V & A Heating was first created in 1996 by Vaughan and Adele Cottam (V&A) and has had the recent addition of their son George Cottam. Offering domestic central heating services to Weston-super-Mare and surrounding areas.
The company has been very successful and has been running on word of mouth since it opened.

What we do

We are registered with Gas Safe to work on almost any domestic gas appliance. We mainly focus on repairing, servicing and installing domestic boilers and heating systems. We are Worcester Bosch accredited installers but again will work on any boiler.
